## Image prompt

> Photorealistic still, 16:9, shot on a Sony Venice cinema camera with a 35mm prime lens. Television cooking competition production aesthetic.
>
> **Composition:** Wide low-angle hero shot, slightly heroic upward tilt. The man [A1] is centered in the frame, walking forward toward camera through the broad doorway entrance of a vast competition kitchen [A2]. Camera is at hip height, looking slightly up at him.
>
> **Subject:** The man [A1] mid-stride, right foot forward, weight committed. He wears a pristine plain white chef's apron tied at the waist with a single bow on his right hip, over a dark grey crew-neck cotton t-shirt with sleeves rolled to mid-forearm, plain dark slim-fit chinos. A black canvas knife roll bag is slung over his right shoulder, held in place by his right hand gripping the strap. White cotton kitchen towel folded crisply, tucked into the apron strings on his right hip. His jaw is set, lips slightly pressed together. Eyes locked forward on the kitchen ahead — focused, alive with adrenaline, but composed. Hair short, neat, brown-black.
>
> **Background:** Around and behind him, slightly out of focus from depth of field, nineteen other contestants in matching white aprons walk forward alongside him in the same direction. They are visible but clearly secondary. Beyond them the full kitchen [A2] reveals itself — 24 stainless-steel cooking stations in two long rows on a polished black floor, copper and stainless cookware racks lining the side walls, industrial truss ceiling 25 feet overhead dense with bare-tube fixtures and softboxes. Far back center, the massive digital countdown clock hangs above the pass, displaying **60:00** in bold orange-red digits on a black face — clearly readable.
>
> **Lighting:** Bright multi-source production lighting. Hard key from overhead truss. Soft kino-flo fill from both sides at chest height. Color temperature ~4800K, slightly cool industrial white. No deep shadows. Skin tone natural, warm, slightly heated. Subtle defined lens flare from the bare-tube overheads.
>
> **Color & focus:** High dynamic range. Vibrant saturation — the orange-red clock numbers pop against the cool steel environment. Sharp focus on him, slight depth-of-field falloff to the contestants behind, but kitchen architecture remains readable. Reference aesthetic: opening sequence of MasterChef US finale.

## Negative prompt

dark moody lighting, deep shadows, dim kitchen, soft focus, motion blur on subject, faded color grade, vignetting, deformed hands or face, extra fingers, contestants in colors other than white aprons, illegible clock, the man in front of the doorway alone (he should be entering with others), painterly stylization.

## Generation notes

- **Generate this frame first.** It establishes the entire film's look. Once locked, every subsequent frame should match its lighting, color grade, and skin tone.
- If the contestants behind him feel too prominent, ask for stronger DoF separation: "background contestants softer, depth of field shallower in middle ground."
- The clock must be readable — if it comes out blurry or cut off, regenerate with "clock display sharply in focus, '60:00' clearly legible" emphasized.
- Save the seed/parameters once locked. You'll want to match grading on F7 as a bookend.
