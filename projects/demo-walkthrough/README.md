# Example: MasterChef-Style Pink Cup Demo

This is a completed example of the storyboard workflow.

The brief:

> Make a cinematic cooking competition sequence with time pressure and a recurring pink cup at the chef's station.

What this example proves:

- The cup repeats across the sequence.
- The chef holds across cuts.
- The kitchen world stays consistent.
- The final video is made from short controlled shots.

## How To Read This Example

For a beginner workshop, copy this finished example into `projects/` first:

```bash
tools/setup-demo.sh demo-walkthrough --open
```

Then open:

```text
projects/demo-walkthrough/
```

Open folders in order:

1. `01-creative-brief/approved/`
2. `02-references/approved/`
3. `03-shot-list/approved/`
4. `04-image-prompts/approved/`
5. `05-storyboard-frames/`
6. `06-video-prompts/approved/`
7. `07-transition-videos/approved/`
8. `08-stitching/approved/`
9. `09-final-output/`

## Approval Pattern

This example keeps both attempts and approved outputs.

The strongest storyboard set was copied into:

`05-storyboard-frames/approved/`

Rejected or not-final materials are kept in:

`disapproved/`

That way a beginner can see what changed and why.
