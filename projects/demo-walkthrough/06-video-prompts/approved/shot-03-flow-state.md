# Shot 03 — Flow State

**Duration:** 10 seconds
**Anchors:** F3 (Pantry Storm) → F4 (The Peak)
**Apron state:** Single olive-oil streak (from F3) → working stains (flour smudges, herb-oil smear, two soot marks) by F4
**Clock:** Falling — 50:00 → 30:00 → 24:13 visible in cuts
**Emotional beat:** Peak confidence / the dance of cooking / private triumph

---

## Frame anchors

**FIRST FRAME (F3):** Man in pantry mid-stride twisting past contestant, arms full of ingredients, single olive-oil streak across chest of pristine apron.

**LAST FRAME (F4):** Medium shot at his station. The pristine plated dish [A4] on the counter — three perfectly seared scallops on cauliflower purée with herb oil drizzle and a single nasturtium flower. His hands are pulled back, fingers spread, hovering above the plate. Small private smile. Apron with working stains. Clock visible behind reads **24:13**.

---

## Universal style block

Photorealistic, broadcast-television cooking-competition aesthetic. Sony Venice cinema camera, 35mm prime lens, 16:9. Bright multi-source production lighting (overhead truss + kino-flo fill, ~4800K, no deep shadows). Skin tones natural. Vibrant saturation. Sharp focus.

## Section 4 — Energy arc

- **Beat 1 (0–1s) — Return:** Sprint back, counter drop, ignition.
- **Beat 2 (1–7s) — Flow:** Six rapid macro cooking cuts. The dance.
- **Beat 3 (7–8s) — Reverence:** The flower placed. Audio drops. Time slows.
- **Beat 4 (8–10s) — Triumph:** Pull-back. Hands hovering. Private smile. The ending of an artist's perfect take.

This is the FIRST emotional peak of the film. The viewer should feel his confidence in their chest.

## Sound design notes

- 0–1s: Sustained chaos from Shot 2 carrying over; ingredients hitting steel.
- 1–4s: The kitchen war recedes. Solo piano enters. Sizzle, knife on board, pan-fire whoosh — Foley elevated above ambient.
- 4–7s: Piano rises with strings. Foley remains close-mic'd: spoon scrape, tweezer click, oil drip.
- 7–8s: AUDIO DROP. Just the placement of the flower. Held breath.
- 8–10s: Score returns subtly, almost a single sustained note of warmth as the camera pulls back. Land quiet.

## Negative prompt

frantic energy in the cooking section (it should be virtuosic, not panicked), dim lighting, deep shadows, soft focus on dish, illegible clock, dish poorly composed (the plate must look Michelin-tier), apron pristine at end (working stains required), motion blur on the dish, multiple plates in frame, raw scallops (they must show golden caramelization), generic restaurant footage, painterly stylization.

## Director's notes

- A4 (the dish) is the entire emotional payoff of this shot. If A4 isn't generated to a Michelin-tier standard, generate it standalone first and lock it.
- The herb-oil drizzle in 3.7 at ~20% speed is the SIGNATURE moment. If Seedance can only land one slow-motion beat well, prioritize this one.
- The smile in 3.9 must be PRIVATE — not a "smiling for the camera" smile. Prompt explicitly: "small subtle smile at the corners of the mouth, eyes locked on plate, contained pride, almost imperceptible." If it reads as theatrical, regenerate.
- Apron progression: it must visibly accumulate stains across the 10 seconds. If Seedance can't simulate progressive staining, generate the shot with the working-stains apron throughout and accept slight continuity smudge — better than getting a clean apron at F4 which would betray the cut.
- The audio drop at 3.8 is a sound-design instruction; visually, just hold a beat of stillness on the flower placement.
