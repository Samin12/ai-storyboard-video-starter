# Risk It or Drink It — TikTok Ad Storyline v1

**Total runtime:** 60s (6 clips × 10s each)
**Format:** 9:16, 1080p, hyper-realistic UGC-feel
**Hook:** "POV: She brought THE game out at the kickback…"
**Tone:** Mischievous, intimate, unhinged-in-a-good-way, TikTok-native
**Sales channel:** TikTok Shop

## Cast
- **Bianca** — host, Black woman, long twisted braids in half-up bun, pink "Bianca Unboxing" t-shirt (locked via character reference sheet)
- **Maya** — friend, hype, big reactions
- **Jas** — friend, the one who actually does the dares
- **Reese** — friend, dramatic, mock-scandalized energy

## Card text used (sourced from real "Do or Drink"–style decks)
- Green: "Try to lick your elbow for 10 seconds."
- Black: "Let the group scroll through your phone for 30 seconds."
- Red: "Text your ex 'I miss you' right now."

---

## Clip 1 — HOOK (0–10s)
**Title:** "She brought THE game out"
**Action:** Bianca walks into the warmly lit living room holding the red and black "Risk It or Drink It" box behind her back. She reveals it dramatically over the coffee table. Maya's eyes go wide; Reese covers her mouth.
**Camera:** Handheld follow as Bianca walks in, then a snap-zoom onto the box as it lands on the coffee table.
**Sound:** Off-camera "ohhhh she didn't" reaction, lo-fi R&B underneath.

## Clip 2 — REVEAL (10–20s)
**Title:** "150 cards. 4 levels. Pure chaos."
**Action:** Macro shot of Bianca's hands cracking the box open. She fans the cards out across the table — visible color-coded backs: white, green, black, red. Friends lean in, hands hovering.
**Camera:** Macro lens close-up of the box opening; rack focus to Jas's grin.
**Sound:** Card slap, plastic crinkle, group "ooooh."

## Clip 3 — GREEN CARD: THE WARM-UP (20–30s)
**Title:** "Easy one to start…"
**Action:** Jas draws a green card. Tight close-up of the card in her hand reading: **"Try to lick your elbow for 10 seconds."** She tries, contorts her face, fails. Group erupts laughing.
**Camera:** Macro on the card text, then handheld push-in on Jas's face mid-attempt; quick cuts of the group laughing.
**Sound:** Laughter, table slap.

## Clip 4 — BLACK CARD: BIANCA'S TURN (30–40s)
**Title:** "Oh she did NOT just—"
**Action:** Bianca draws a black card. Close-up reveals: **"Let the group scroll through your phone for 30 seconds."** She gasps, looks at her phone, looks at the camera, slams the card down, grabs a shot glass, and downs it instead. Reese throws her hands up.
**Camera:** Macro on card text → rack focus to Bianca's "no shot" face → handheld on the shot going down.
**Sound:** Group "OHHHH," ice clink, glass slam on table.

## Clip 5 — RED CARD: THE LIMIT (40–50s)
**Title:** "Red card. The energy shifts."
**Action:** Maya slowly slides a red card off the deck. Tight close-up on the card: **"Text your ex 'I miss you' right now."** Group goes feral — screams, hands on heads, Reese standing up. Maya stares at her phone.
**Camera:** Slow macro reveal of the card text; tracking shot panning across friends' shocked reactions.
**Sound:** Collective scream, lo-fi music swells, phone vibration.

## Clip 6 — DECISION + HERO SHOT (50–60s)
**Title:** "She risked it."
**Action:** Maya's finger hovers over the "send" button. Beat. She slams the phone face-down on the table, picks up the shot, downs it. Group erupts cheering. Camera pulls back and pushes in on the "Risk It or Drink It" box centered on the coffee table, group laughing in soft focus behind it.
**Camera:** ECU on phone screen → cut to wide reaction → slow dolly push-in on hero box.
**Sound:** Cheers, glass clink, music drops, then ambient laughter under final beat.
