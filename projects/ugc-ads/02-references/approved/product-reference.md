# Locked Product Reference

## Product
**Dr. Berg D3 & K2 Vitamin** — 2-in-1 immunity & bone-support supplement.

## Specs (read off the bottle in product-front.jpg)
- 10,000 IU Vitamin D3 per serving
- 100 mcg Vitamin K2 per serving
- 120 Capsules per bottle
- Manufactured in the USA
- Brand: Dr. Berg ("The Knowledge Doc")
- Dosage: 1 capsule daily

## Benefits to dramatize on screen
1. **Immunity support** — D3 supports normal immune function.
2. **Bone & teeth health** — D3 + K2 work together; K2 directs calcium to bones, not arteries.
3. **Mood & energy** — D3 supports healthy mood and reduces fatigue.
4. **2-in-1 convenience** — replaces multiple separate D and K supplements.
5. **High-dose absorption** — 10,000 IU is a clinically meaningful dose; pairing with K2 boosts utilization.
