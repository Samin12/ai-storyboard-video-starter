# Shot List with Dialogue — "Why I Switched to One Bottle"

**Format:** 9:16 vertical · 6 shots · ~5 seconds each · ~30 seconds total
**Creator:** female, late 20s–early 30s, warm, conversational. Same outfit, same kitchen, morning light in every shot.

---

## Shot 1 — HOOK (0:00–0:05)
**Visual:** Tight close-up. Creator holds the Dr. Berg bottle right up to camera with a "you're not gonna believe this" look. Slight zoom-in. Out-of-focus kitchen behind her.
**Camera:** Front-facing phone, slight tilt, hand-held wobble.
**Dialogue (creator, fast, surprised):**
> "Wait — I was taking FIVE different vitamins every morning... until I found this one bottle."

**Beat purpose:** scroll-stopper. Bottle on screen by second 1.

---

## Shot 2 — PROBLEM (0:05–0:10)
**Visual:** Wide overhead shot of her kitchen counter cluttered with 6–8 generic, unbranded supplement bottles. Her hand sweeps across them in mock frustration.
**Camera:** Top-down, daylight, real kitchen mess.
**Dialogue (creator, voice-over):**
> "Vitamin D, K2, magnesium, zinc, B-complex — my counter looked like a pharmacy. And I never even remembered which one to take."

**Beat purpose:** make the viewer say "yep, same."

---

## Shot 3 — REVEAL (0:10–0:15)
**Visual:** Medium shot. Creator centered, holding the Dr. Berg D3 & K2 bottle at chest height, label squared to camera. She's smiling, confident.
**Camera:** Eye-level, tripod-steady this time, label clearly readable.
**Dialogue (creator, calm, direct):**
> "Then I found Dr. Berg's D3 and K2. Ten thousand IU of D, plus K2 — both in one capsule."

**Beat purpose:** clean product hero. Label must be 100% legible.

---

## Shot 4 — DEMONSTRATION (0:15–0:20)
**Visual:** Close-up of her hand tipping one capsule from the bottle into her palm, then bringing a glass of water up. Soft focus on bottle in background.
**Camera:** Down-tilt, hands and capsule in sharp focus, bottle visible but soft.
**Dialogue (creator, casual):**
> "One capsule a day. That's it. Immunity, bones, mood, energy — all covered."

**Beat purpose:** show how easy. Real human moment.

---

## Shot 5 — PROOF (0:20–0:25)
**Visual:** The bottle on a windowsill with a strong morning sun beam hitting the orange label. Slow push-in (no creator in this shot — pure product hero).
**Camera:** Slow dolly-in, bokeh window light, almost cinematic but still phone-shot.
**Dialogue (creator, voice-over):**
> "10,000 IU is the dose that actually does something. Made in the USA. 120 capsules — that's a four-month supply."

**Beat purpose:** credibility + value framing.

---

## Shot 6 — CTA (0:25–0:30)
**Visual:** Creator back on camera, holding the bottle next to her face, big smile, pointing at it.
**Camera:** Front-facing, eye-level, slightly playful tilt.
**Dialogue (creator, friendly nudge):**
> "Stop wasting money on five separate bottles. Link's in my bio — go grab one."

**Beat purpose:** clear, low-pressure CTA. Smile-out close.

---

## Continuity checklist
- [ ] Same creator face/hair/outfit in shots 1, 3, 4, 6.
- [ ] Same bottle, same label orientation in shots 1, 3, 4, 5, 6.
- [ ] Same kitchen/lighting/environment throughout.
- [ ] Vertical 9:16 framing on every shot.
